🤵🏻 About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Nanjing University and a member of the LAMDA Group, within the School of Artificial Intelligence. I am advised by Professor Yu-Feng Li, and previously I worked closely with Prof. Lei Feng. My current research mainly focuses on spatial intelligence, especially how learning systems understand, reason about, and act on structured spatial information. I also work on neuro-symbolic AI and trustworthy AI, with an interest in combining large language models, symbolic tools, and reliable reasoning.
